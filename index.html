关于我们 About Us

清华大学学生游泳协会（泳协），是经校团委批准，依托体育部和游泳馆建立的正规学生社团。泳协以推广游泳运动、丰富学生课余生活为核心，主要开展各类群众游泳活动，同时作为马杯游泳比赛的承办协会，为热爱游泳的同学提供交流竞技、提升技能的优质平台。 The Tsinghua University Student Swimming Association (TSSA) is an official student organization approved by the Youth League Committee, operating under the Department of Physical Education and the Swimming Center. Our mission is to promote swimming as a sport and enrich students' extracurricular life. As the organizer of the Ma Cup Swimming Competition, we provide a platform for swimming enthusiasts to connect, compete, and improve their skills.
